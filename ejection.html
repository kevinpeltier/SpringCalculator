PINS

  • There may be times when a small ejector pin may be needed. However, the clearance cannot be smaller than Ø 0.1875.

  • Any ejector pin clearance above Ø 0.1875 needs to be 0.625" greater than the ejector pin diameter.
    Cimatron default is 0.03125 larger than the pin, this will need to be manually changed.

  • No ream for return pins, core block clearance all the way through.
