Standard Lifter Bushing Diameters:
  • 1.00"
  • 0.750"
  • 25mm
  • 20mm
  • All other diameters of bushings must be review with the shop floor prior to final.

Max Drilling Depths
  • Preferred Max Depth: 10.00".
  • Max drilling depth for lifter bushings as dictated by shop tooling is 14.0" with 10.0" being the preferred max.
  • If the depth is greater than 14.0", bushings must be installed from both sides.
  • If these standards cannot be achieved, manager approval is required.
